## Welcome to Moss

Moss is an AI-powered assistant platform that embeds directly into your web application. It provides contextual help, step-by-step guidance, and intelligent support - helping your users navigate and accomplish tasks seamlessly.

### Key Capabilities

- **Contextual Understanding** - Moss sees what your users see, providing relevant assistance based on their current screen and actions
- **Step-by-Step Guidance** - Break down complex tasks into manageable steps with visual highlighting
- **Knowledge Base Integration** - Connect your documentation for accurate, up-to-date answers
- **Session Replay** - Review user interactions to improve your product and support
